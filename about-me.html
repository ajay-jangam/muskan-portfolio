I am a designer who approaches design like creating the perfect recipe. Just like in cooking, I mix creativity, strategy, and a dash of intuition to craft digital experiences that feel just right! From fintech and agri-tech to SaaS and B2C products, every project is a new dish, blending user needs with business goals to create something innovative.

When I’m not designing, you’ll find me experimenting in the kitchen, decorating my space, painting canvases, or on the road taking long scenic drives. I’m also a nature enthusiast, always capturing the beauty of the world through my camera. These passions keep my creativity alive and deeply influence my design process.

Design, to me, is more than just how something looks, it’s how it makes you feel, much like how the right meal brings comfort. Whether it's in a UX of an app’s navigation or the smallest UI detail, I believe every element should feel intuitive and personal, like a space you want to keep coming back to.
